SECTION 3 — PROPOSED SOLUTION
================================================================================

We will build a custom AI automation system that handles the first 72 hours of
every lead relationship without any manual work from your team.

The system has three layers:

LAYER 1 — CAPTURE (responds in under 60 seconds)
  AI voice agent answers every inbound call 24/7
  Web form triggers instant SMS to the prospect
  Missed call triggers automated text-back within 10 seconds
  After-hours inquiries are captured and sorted for morning review

LAYER 2 — QUALIFY (happens automatically)
  Voice agent asks qualification questions (service type, location, timeline)
  Responses logged to CRM with lead score
  Hot leads flagged for immediate human follow-up
  Cold leads enter automated nurture sequence

LAYER 3 — FOLLOW UP (runs for 30 days without intervention)
  Touch 1: Immediate response (under 60 seconds)
  Touch 2: Follow-up SMS at 24 hours if no reply
  Touch 3: Email follow-up at 48 hours
  Touch 4: Second SMS at day 5
  Touch 5: Final email at day 14
  All stops automatically when prospect replies or books
