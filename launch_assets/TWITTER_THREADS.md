## THREAD 2 — The Kelly Criterion

1/ The #1 reason traders blow up isn't bad entries.

It's bad position sizing.

Here's the math that fixed mine: 🧵

2/ Kelly Criterion formula:

f = (bp - q) / b

- b = avg win / avg loss
- p = win rate
- q = 1 - p
- f = fraction of account to risk

Most traders skip this. That's the mistake.

3/ Full Kelly is too aggressive.

Institutions use Quarter Kelly (f × 0.25).

At 55% win rate and 1.5:1 win/loss, Quarter Kelly says risk ~2.3% per trade.

Not 10%. Not 0.5%. 2.3%.
