⚡ 15-Minute Script (UPDATED — High Urgency + Free Trial)
⏱️ Minute 0–2: Frame + Stakes

“Hey — I’ll keep this quick.
In the next 10–15 minutes I’ll show you where time and money are slipping, and how we can turn this into something that runs consistently — especially with things being uncertain on your main job. Sound fair?”

(You’ve now anchored urgency + relevance to his situation)

⏱️ Minute 2–5: Extract Pain (Side Business Angle)

Ask:

“Right now, how many jobs are you realistically missing just because you’re busy or can’t respond fast enough?”
“If your main job disappeared — could this business fully carry you as it is right now?”
“What’s the biggest bottleneck — time, follow-up, or organization?”

Then:

“So it’s not really a lead problem — it’s a system problem.”

⏱️ Minute 5–8: Reframe (Make it Critical)

“What you have right now works as a side hustle —
but it’s not structured to support you if it had to become your main income.”

Pause.

“That’s the gap we fix.”

⏱️ Minute 8–11: Present Solution (AUTOMATION = TIME BACK)

“What I’d set up for you is a system that basically runs the front-end of your business for you.”

Then list ONLY outcomes:

Every lead gets responded to instantly
No one falls through the cracks
You don’t have to chase people
Everything is organized automatically
You only step in when it actually matters

Then drop this:

“We can even plug in automated call/text handling so you’re not glued to your phone.”

(Perfect place to mention your system casually — not as a pitch, as proof)

⏱️ Minute 11–13: Offer + Free Trial (LOW RESISTANCE CLOSE)

“Normally I build this out as a full system for $3,500 — done in about a week.”

Then immediately:

“But here’s what I’ll do —
I’ll set up a working version of this for you first so you can actually see it in action.”

Pause.

“If it’s doing what it’s supposed to do, we move forward. If not, no pressure.”

🔥 This is your kill shot:

“There’s really no reason not to at least test it — especially with where things are at right now.”

⏱️ Minute 13–15: Close

“If we start this week, I can have something live for you within a few days for testing.”

Then:

“Do you want me to start putting that together for you?”

Stop talking. Let them answer.
