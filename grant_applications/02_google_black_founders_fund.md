## APPLICATION ESSAY: WHAT DOES YOUR COMPANY DO?
*(300 words max — paste directly)*

Gray Horizons Enterprise builds AI automation infrastructure for two underserved markets simultaneously.

We build the complete automation stack: GoHighLevel CRM workflows, AI voice agents that answer and book inbound calls 24/7, and 14-day lead nurture sequences that run without any human intervention. Both products are built on a single architecture: AI that replaces manual judgment with systematic process. One side serves businesses. The other serves individual investors. The same founder built both.

The market opportunity is not theoretical. 88% of businesses have adopted AI tools. Only 6% are using them effectively. That gap — between adoption and competence — is where Gray Horizons Enterprise operates.

## APPLICATION ESSAY: WHY DO YOU NEED FUNDING?
*(200-300 words — paste directly)*

Gray Horizons Enterprise does not have a product problem. The systems are built, tested, and ready to deploy. The constraint is runway.

Every dollar currently spent on platform subscriptions, hosting, and API costs comes directly from the founder. There is no investor. There is no safety net. The business is one bad month away from having to shut down the infrastructure while actively trying to close the first paying client.

The $50,000-$100,000 award would solve this in three specific ways.

First, it eliminates the financial pressure that compresses the sales cycle. When survival is the daily objective, every prospect conversation carries desperation. Funded founders sell differently. They qualify properly. They walk away from bad fits. They close better clients.

Second, it funds the production of the assets that close deals: demo videos of the AI voice agent live on a call, case study documentation from real client deployments, and a proper content engine that drives inbound leads rather than cold outreach.

Third, it allows the founder to pay a living wage to himself during the 90 days required to reach the first milestone: 8 retainer clients at $750/month, which generates $6,000 monthly recurring revenue and makes the business self-sustaining without external capital.

This is a bridge, not a crutch. The business model works. The technology works. The market is real. The funding is the 90-day window between where we are and where this becomes a company that funds itself.

We are building where the competition is not.
