## NOTES ON OWNER PAY

**Can you pay yourself from grant funds?**

YES — in most of these programs:

- **Hello Alice:** Unrestricted cash award. You can use it however the business needs it.
- **Google Black Founders Fund:** Unrestricted cash award. No reporting on use required.
- **Verizon Digital Ready:** Unrestricted. No restrictions on how funds are spent.
- **CA CEDAP:** Allows "project labor" — the founder's time spent on the project counts as an expense.
- **NSF SBIR:** PI (Principal Investigator) salary is a required, standard budget line item. The founder can pay themselves a market-rate salary from Phase I funds. This is not optional — it is expected.

The NSF SBIR is the most important one on this point. A $275,000 Phase I award can include $85,000+ in PI salary over 12 months. That is the single highest-value outcome in this plan.
